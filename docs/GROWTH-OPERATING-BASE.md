# Growth Operating Base

This is the default pack for taking a product from MVP to adoption, retention, and growth.
It keeps research, measurement, onboarding, pricing, experiments, and launch loops aligned.

## Required Docs

Every product that is entering beta, launch, or growth work should have:

- customer research notes
- analytics and measurement plan
- onboarding and activation plan
- pricing and packaging plan
- experimentation plan
- launch plan
- support feedback loop

## Research

- collect customer interviews, support themes, and direct feedback
- use actual customer language, not invented wording
- segment by role, use case, and market when possible
- update research when the market or product changes materially

## Measurement

- define the key conversion, activation, and retention events
- track only the events that inform decisions
- keep naming consistent across products
- verify that analytics are actually firing before relying on them

## Onboarding And Activation

- define the aha moment
- remove unnecessary steps between signup and first value
- keep the first session focused on one outcome
- measure drop-off and fix the worst step first

## Pricing And Packaging

- choose a value metric that matches how customers get value
- keep pricing simple enough to explain quickly
- test price changes before making major shifts when possible
- keep packaging aligned with product tiers and support levels

## Experimentation

- test one meaningful change at a time when possible
- define the hypothesis before launch
- choose a primary metric and guardrail metrics
- do not treat a test as complete until the method and result are documented

## Launch

- build a launch plan for the product or feature
- prepare owned, rented, and borrowed channels only as needed
- ship with a clear message, proof points, and call to action
- capture signups, interest, and feedback into owned channels

## Support Feedback Loop

- route support themes back into product and onboarding
- track recurring friction
- keep a short list of top issues that affect activation or retention
- close the loop on what changed because of feedback

## Rules

- growth work should improve the product, not distort it
- do not optimize for vanity metrics
- keep the growth stack small until the product has traction
- document every major growth change in the project context or release notes

## Rule

If a product is going to market, add the growth operating base before beta or full launch.
