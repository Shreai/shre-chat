# Terms and Conditions

> Template only. This is an operating document, not legal advice.
> Each product should be reviewed by qualified counsel for the jurisdictions where it operates.

## Purpose

This document defines the baseline terms for products delivered through Shre OS unless a customer-specific contract overrides it.

## Core Sections

- acceptance of terms
- account registration and security
- acceptable use and prohibited conduct
- service changes and availability
- fees, billing, refunds, and disputes
- intellectual property and license scope
- third-party services and integrations
- suspension and termination
- disclaimers and limitation of liability
- indemnity, where applicable
- governing law and venue
- changes to these terms

## Operating Rules

- use product-specific terms when a client contract requires them
- do not copy governing-law language across nations without review
- keep fee and refund language aligned with the actual commercial agreement
- keep SLA language separate from legal warranty language
- version every substantive update

## Review Triggers

- launch in a new country or region
- collection of regulated data
- consumer-facing billing or subscriptions
- white-label delivery to a customer brand
- any product that includes arbitration, class-action waiver, or similar clauses
