# Privacy Policy

> Template only. This is an operating document, not legal advice.
> Local privacy obligations can differ by country, state, province, or industry.

## Purpose

This document defines the baseline privacy rules for products built on Shre OS.

## Typical Topics

- what data is collected
- why data is collected
- how data is used
- legal basis or notice model where required
- cookies and analytics
- sharing with service providers and subprocessors
- retention and deletion
- data subject or consumer rights
- international transfers
- security practices
- children's data, if applicable
- contact and privacy request process

## Operating Rules

- collect the minimum data needed for the product
- keep privacy notices aligned with actual product behavior
- disclose analytics, messaging, and third-party sharing plainly
- separate internal telemetry from customer-visible data when possible
- keep country-specific notice language versioned when required

## Review Triggers

- new signup flow
- new analytics or ad tracking
- new third-party processor
- cross-border data transfer
- new regulated data class
