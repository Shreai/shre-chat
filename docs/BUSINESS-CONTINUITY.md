# Business Continuity

This document covers recovery planning for outages, incidents, and major disruptions.

## Purpose

- keep the business running through disruption
- define recovery targets
- assign crisis ownership
- make failover and restoration testable

## Core Fields

- system or product
- owner
- critical functions
- recovery time objective
- recovery point objective
- backup location
- failover path
- restore path
- crisis contact
- test date

## Rules

- define RTO and RPO for important products
- test restores and failover paths on a schedule
- document who can declare an incident and who can approve recovery steps
- keep customer communications ready for high-severity events
- tie continuity back to backup, incident, and rollout docs

## Coverage

- data backup and restore
- service failover
- crisis communications
- dependency substitution
- operating in degraded mode

## Rule

If a product is expected to stay live, it needs a continuity plan.
