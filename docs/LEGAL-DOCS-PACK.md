# Legal Docs Pack

## Include When Needed

- Terms and Conditions
- Privacy Policy
- Copyright and IP Policy
- Jurisdiction Matrix
- Data Processing Agreement
- Business Associate Agreement
- Subprocessor list
- Acceptable Use Policy
- Security addendum

## Rules

- use customer-specific legal docs when required
- do not imply legal coverage that has not been reviewed
- align any regulated product claims with the actual agreement in place
- treat country-specific legal requirements as jurisdiction-specific, not universal
- route any new market launch or regulated-data launch through legal review before release
- keep terms, privacy, IP, and governing-law language versioned per product or tenant when needed
